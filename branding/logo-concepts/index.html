Six directions for the Makers Co mark

Same orbit-and-core identity, reworked with the depth, gradient, and craft of a premium 2026 tech brand. Pick a direction, mix elements, or request another round.

Concept 1

Refined Orbit

The safest evolution — same three-ring atom mark, rebuilt with a continuous holographic gradient, a true glass core with specular highlight, and a tighter, bolder wordmark. Closest to the current brand, just elevated.

Fabric Backdrop
